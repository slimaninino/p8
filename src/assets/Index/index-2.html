About the lounge

Thoughtful hospitality, not just another party room.

Each booking is shaped around privacy, comfort, and elevated hosting. Guests move from welcome drinks to curated room service and seamless song access without the noise and chaos of an open karaoke bar.

That makes the concept ideal for executive gatherings, anniversaries, premium birthdays, and social groups who want exclusivity without losing the fun.

Services

High-touch details across the evening

Rather than stacking generic features, the lounge experience is presented as a series of premium service moments from arrival to final encore.

Arrival

Hosted room welcome

Guests are received with a prepared suite, cold towels, and a beverage-first start that feels closer to a lounge than a late-night venue.

During service

Curated food and drinks

Champagne, wine, signature cocktails, and share plates arrive tableside without interrupting the session flow.

Personalization

Song and event tailoring

Personal title screens, mood lighting, and custom song queues help private events feel bespoke instead of pre-packaged.
